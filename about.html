About BB Welding & Engineering

BB Welding & Engineering provides high-quality mobile welding and engineering solutions across Dunmow and Essex. We bring our workshop to you, offering flexibility, precision, and speed for every project.

From custom metalwork to structural repairs, our mobile services mean you never have to worry about transporting heavy or bulky items. Serving Dunmow, Essex and surrounding areas, our team ensures each project meets the highest standards.
